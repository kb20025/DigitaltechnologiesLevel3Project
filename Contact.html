Our Mission

As an 18-year-old student with a deep passion for global topics, I’ve come to realize that understanding the history of the world is not just important—it's essential for the future. I believe that New Zealand, like many other nations, stands to benefit immensely from a greater awareness of global history. Without this knowledge, we risk repeating the same mistakes that have shaped past conflicts, struggles, and failures. History teaches us critical lessons about governance, diplomacy, and human rights, and by embracing it, we can build a more informed and resilient future for our country.
