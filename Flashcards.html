What is the largest desert in the world?

The Antarctic Desert

Covering an area of 14 million square kilometers, the Antarctic Desert is the largest desert on Earth.

Which country has the longest coastline?

Canada

Canada's coastline stretches over 202,080 kilometers, making it the longest in the world.

What is the oldest known civilization?

The Sumerians

The Sumerians established one of the world's first civilizations in Mesopotamia around 3500 BC.

Which continent is known as the "Dark Continent"?

Africa

Africa was historically referred to as the "Dark Continent" due to the lack of knowledge and understanding about it.

What is the capital city of Australia?

Canberra

Canberra was selected as the capital of Australia in 1908 as a compromise between rivals Sydney and Melbourne.

Which river is the longest in the world?

The Nile River

The Nile River flows over 6,650 kilometers and is traditionally regarded as the longest river in the world.

What is the smallest country in the world?

Vatican City

Vatican City is an independent city-state enclaved within Rome, Italy, and is the smallest country in the world.

Which ancient wonder was located in Babylon?

The Hanging Gardens

The Hanging Gardens of Babylon are considered one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, though their existence is debated.

What is the longest river in Asia?

The Yangtze River

Flowing over 6,300 kilometers, the Yangtze River is the longest river in Asia and the third-longest in the world.

Which continent is home to the Amazon Rainforest?

South America

The Amazon Rainforest is primarily located in Brazil and is the largest tropical rainforest in the world.

What is the highest mountain in the world?

Mount Everest

Mount Everest, located in the Himalayas, reaches an elevation of 8,848 meters above sea level.

Which ocean is the largest?

The Pacific Ocean

The Pacific Ocean covers more than 63 million square miles, making it the largest ocean on Earth.

What is the capital of Egypt?

Cairo

Cairo is the capital city of Egypt and is known for its rich history, including the nearby Pyramids of Giza.

What is the most populous country in the world?

China

China has the largest population of any country, with over 1.4 billion residents.

What ancient civilization built the pyramids?

The Egyptians

The ancient Egyptians constructed the pyramids as tombs for their pharaohs during the Old and Middle Kingdoms.

What is the primary language spoken in Brazil?

Portuguese

Portuguese is the official language of Brazil, making it the largest Portuguese-speaking country in the world.

Which continent is known for its diverse wildlife?

Australia

Australia is famous for its unique wildlife, including kangaroos, koalas, and the Great Barrier Reef.

Which empire was known for its road system?

The Roman Empire

The Roman Empire is renowned for its extensive network of roads, which facilitated trade and military movement.

What is the largest island in the world?

Greenland

Greenland is the largest island in the world, covering an area of about 2.166 million square kilometers.

What ancient city is known as the "Rose City"?

Petra

Petra, located in Jordan, is called the "Rose City" due to the color of the stone from which it is carved.

Which country invented paper?

China

Paper was invented in China during the Han Dynasty around 105 AD by Cai Lun.
